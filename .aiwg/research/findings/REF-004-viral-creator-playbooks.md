# REF-004 — Viral Creator Playbooks ($100k+/mo operators)

Full structured agent output preserved in this file's source run (Firecrawl job 019f2e79-74ff-758b-b51c-389a01750d3d). Key findings below, hedged per GRADE.

## 1. Hook systems (strongest evidence — operator-stated)

- **Jenny Hoyos** (600M views/yr, avg 10M/video): hook + foreshadowing in ≤3s; first 3s must communicate value **without audio**; 5th-grade readability; power words ("banned, free, one dollar, secret, cheap"); target length exactly 34s; retention floor 90% (100%+ via rewatch for <30s). ([marketingexamined](https://www.marketingexamined.com/blog/jenny-hoyos-short-form-video-playbook), [creator science pod](https://podcast.creatorscience.com/jenny-hoyos/))
- Pattern-interrupt hook taxonomy: bold claim / curiosity gap / micro-story / visual shock / direct question; spoken word in first 0.5s, visual hook ≤1.5-2s. ([virvid](https://virvid.ai/blog/first-3-seconds-hook-faceless-shorts-2026))
- First-frame on-screen text so topic reads on mute; captions from word one (+18-25% watch time reported).

## 2. Retention editing (directional benchmarks)

- Cut/pattern-interrupt every 3-5s; jump cuts 0.8-1.5s in value zones; ≤3s without a text overlay or audio event.
- **Loop construction is a ranking lever**: rewatch 15-22% with engineered interrupts vs 5-8% without ([edicionvideopro](https://edicionvideopro.com/en/editing-for-platforms-video-marketing/pattern-interrupts-tiktok-retention-guide/)).
- "But/Therefore" story chaining (Hoyos); kill dead-air phrases; end on twist/question for replay.
- Viral thresholds (operator-reported): <30s shorts need ~100%+ retention (loops); 60s+ need 80-90%; TikTok aggressive boost ≈70%+ completion.

## 3. Trend-jacking speed (moderate confidence)

- Top pages ride moments in <3h; AI-assisted workflows reported at <40min turnaround; trending sounds valuable in first 24h. ([amraandelma stats](https://www.amraandelma.com/trendjacking-campaign-statistics/), [socialync](https://www.socialync.io/blog/tiktok-algorithm-2026-what-works-now))
- Trendjacking EMV ~$6.40 per $1 vs $2.70 standard branded (industry-reported, VERY LOW grade).
- **Implication for factory: time-from-trend-to-publish is a core KPI; our generation pipeline must hit <3h on breaking WC moments.**

## 4. Posting systems

- Reels 3-5/wk baseline; TikTok 1-2/day; avoid burst-spam; **Hormozi 70/20/10** (70% proven winners / 20% iterations / 10% experiments) at 300+ pieces/wk from one long-form source. ([thibodeau](https://marcjthibodeau.com/insights/hormozi-300-content-per-week))
- Portfolio: prove ONE account to working monetization before cloning (Tai Lopez model: 1 face account + 8 faceless theme pages).
- First 60-90 min post-publish ≈ primary distribution window.

## 5. Monetization at scale (mixed grade)

- Brand deals ≈ 70% of creator income; mega-tier (1M+) IG posts $10k-$250k+. Platform bonuses are small (IG ~$10-90 per 1M views; TikTok Creator Rewards $400-1,000/1M for 60s+ videos; YT Shorts $30-200/1M). **Views alone don't pay — views are the audience-acquisition engine for brand deals/affiliate/products.**
- DM-automation funnels: 300-400% conversion lift vs bio links (single-source, LOW grade) — validates the comment-keyword CTA patterns from the liked-content study.
- YouTube long-form remains the RPM king ($2k-25k/1M) — consistent with our clip-farming analysis.

## 6. Algorithm signals (HIGHEST confidence — platform-stated)

- **Mosseri/Instagram**: watch time, likes-per-reach, DM shares = top 3; **DM sends weigh 3-5x likes** for non-follower reach; "sends per reach" is THE metric; ≥3% sends/reach = viral territory. Initial audition pool 200-1,000 non-followers; first 90 min decisive. ([creators.instagram.com](https://creators.instagram.com/grow/algorithms-and-ranking), [socialync shares guide](https://www.socialync.io/blog/instagram-shares-algorithm-complete-guide-2026))
- **TikTok**: completion + rewatch dominant (est. 40-50% weight); follower count NOT a ranking factor (official).
- Optimal lengths: TikTok max-virality 11-18s; monetization requires 60s+; IG discovery <30s.

## 7. ⚠ Adverse findings (do not bury)

1. **Instagram reportedly deprioritizes AI-generated content** vs "raw, real human content" (industry-reported, LOW grade — but directionally consistent across sources). Mitigations: human-feel VO, imperfect handheld grammar, comedic/parody framing (the player-amplified AI-meme lane thrives DESPITE this), shares-optimized formats (shares signal overrides content-type priors).
2. **Meme pages rank LOWEST in direct-monetization ceiling** among faceless niches (12+ months to first dollar). Consistent with our model: meme lane = audience acquisition; revenue lands via brand deals/affiliate/products/B2B (fly-through lane), not platform bonuses.
3. Watermarks (TikTok/CapCut) and repost-heavy accounts get downranked — publish native files per platform, never cross-watermarked.

## Factory-relevant synthesis

Views→income conversion demands: (a) shares-per-reach as our #1 creative KPI, (b) loop-engineered sub-30s cuts for discovery + 60s+ cuts for TikTok monetization eligibility, (c) <3h trend-response capability, (d) 70/20/10 batch composition, (e) revenue design around brand/affiliate/products — platform payouts are rounding errors.
