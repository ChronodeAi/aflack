# Skill: virality-review

Purpose: compare a draft video package against active insights and benchmark videos before spend.

Gate outcome:
- PASS: package references at least one active insight and has a measurable hypothesis.
- REVISE: hook/structure/CTA weak or untraceable.
- BLOCK: compliance or source-rights issue.

Review checks:
- benchmark insight is named or cited,
- hook has a concrete first-3-second claim,
- CTA has one action and one conversion destination,
- lead magnet, link hub, owned offer, or affiliate path is explicit,
- rumor/speculation wording is compliant for GTA6-adjacent content,
- no paid generation proceeds without the spend gate.
