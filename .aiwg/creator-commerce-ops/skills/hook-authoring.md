# Skill: hook-authoring

Purpose: create short-form hooks that are evidence-bound, compliant, and built
to route attention into the funnel.

Inputs:
- niche,
- target platform,
- active benchmark insights,
- offer or lead magnet,
- compliance constraints.

Required checklist:
- Open with a concrete personal claim, visible outcome, or sharp comparison in
  the first 3 seconds.
- Do not use a logo intro.
- Keep the first line specific enough to be falsifiable.
- Match the hook to one viewer action, not multiple competing CTAs.
- Prefer a one-word comment CTA when the goal is lead capture.
- Make the lead magnet or owned destination the real conversion asset.
- Cite at least one benchmark insight or mark the hook as experimental.

GTA6-safe patterns:
- "GTA6 is not out yet; here is the original AI workflow I built around the
  hype window."
- "This is speculation/commentary, not leaked gameplay."
- "Original AI visuals only; no Rockstar footage or affiliation."

Blocked patterns:
- claims of insider access,
- claims of leaked build/gameplay,
- recreated official footage,
- hooks that imply Rockstar or Take-Two endorsement,
- wealth or revenue claims without observable proof.

Output:
- 5 hook options,
- selected winner,
- rationale tied to active insights,
- CTA keyword or conversion path,
- compliance notes.
