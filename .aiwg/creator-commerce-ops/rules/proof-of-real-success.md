# Rule: proof-of-real-success

Do not treat a creator as a model to learn from merely because they show large numbers, luxury flexing, or claimed revenue.

A creator becomes `verified` only when at least three evidence signals are present:

1. observable engagement rate >= 2%,
2. observable monetization path (affiliate, own product, course, sponsor, platform payout, etc.),
3. sustained cadence >= 14 days.

Follower count alone never verifies a creator. Wealth claims are notes, not proof.
