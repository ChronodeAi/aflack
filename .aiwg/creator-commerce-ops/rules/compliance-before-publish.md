# Rule: compliance-before-publish

No content may be submitted for public publishing unless it passes:

- source provenance check,
- AI/synthetic disclosure check,
- affiliate disclosure check,
- no false firsthand/access/leak claims,
- no prohibited medical/health claims,
- no Rockstar/GTA footage reuse or same-seed remixing.

GTA6/Rockstar-specific guardrail:
- do not claim leaked gameplay, official access, insider access, or first-hand
  play experience,
- do not imply affiliation with Rockstar Games or Take-Two Interactive,
- rewrite leak/official-gameplay framing into clearly labeled rumor,
  speculation, commentary, or creator-workflow framing,
- keep visuals original and AI-generated unless the operator separately
  approves a rights-cleared source asset.

Draft preview is allowed; public publish is human-gated.
