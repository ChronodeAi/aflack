### 20-30s retention skeleton (from liked patterns)
1. **0-2s** — shock claim / contrarian frame
2. **2-6s** — contrarian setup ("not from X — from Y")
3. **6-15s** — the method or theory, in steps
4. **15-24s** — one concrete example/proof beat
5. **24-30s** — CTA (keyword comment, follow-for-Phase-2, or affiliate link per lane)

### Serialization (retention across clips, not just within)
- "Phase 1: the clue. Phase 2: the theory. Phase 3: the payoff." — lordwinanim's structure maps directly onto trailer-analysis arcs for Vice Signal.
- End clips with a forward promise ("Part 2 shows the method") instead of a closed loop.

### CTA formulas
- "Comment 'GTA' and I'll send the breakdown." (comment-volume driver; requires DM automation before use)
- "Follow — Phase 2 drops tomorrow." (serialized follow driver, zero infra needed)
- Loadout Lab lane: link-in-bio gear list, disclosed affiliate.

## ⚠ Compliance adaptation notes (required reading before use)

Several liked patterns conflict with our persona ethics policy (`.aiwg/marketing/strategy/gta6-personas.md`) if copied literally. Adapt as follows:

| Liked pattern | Problem | Compliant adaptation |
|---|---|---|
| "GTA 6 is making me RICH" | False firsthand claim (policy: none) | "GTA6 hype is creating a content gold rush" — claim about the market, not fake personal results |
| "DELETING IN 24H" | Fake scarcity; erodes trust, borderline deceptive | Real urgency instead: "138 days left", "before Trailer 3 drops" — the countdown is genuinely scarce |
| "This feels illegal" | Fine structurally | Keep — curiosity framing, no false claim |
| Comment-keyword → DM automation | Needs DM infra + can read as spam | Defer until DM automation exists; use follow-for-Part-2 meanwhile |
| "I played it / insider info" energy | Policy: no false insider claims, no leaked footage | Frame as analysis: "Rockstar hid this in the beach scene" (verifiable trailer detail) |

**Net rule**: inherit the *psychology* (curiosity gap, urgency, named systems, serialization, unfair-advantage framing) — reject the *fabrications* (fake scarcity, fake results, fake insider status). GTA6's real countdown gives us honest urgency for free.
