## TIKTOK-NATIVE PATTERNS

1. **Search-result SEO is much more explicit than IG.**  
   Captions stuff exact phrases: “FIFA World Cup 2026,” “goal today,” “Round of 16,” “Argentina vs Cape Verde,” “France vs Paraguay,” “WorldCup2026.” TikTok creators are clearly trying to rank in live search, not just hit For You.

2. **“Top liked” labels matter.**  
   TikTok search visibly marks some results as “Top liked,” which creates a hierarchy inside search that IG Reels does not expose as plainly.

3. **Editor-credit culture is unusually public.**  
   Captions like “Upload Method > @wizardftb1,” “thanks for rendering,” and “project file in bio” show a creator subculture where editing methods are part of the content economy.

4. **Football brainrot captions are standardized.**  
   “💀🔥,” “☠️,” “what a match,” “last chance,” “saves Portugal,” “is coming,” “dictator Mbappe,” and “robbed” are reusable TikTok-native emotional templates.

5. **AI/fake-news-adjacent football skits are blended with real match keywords.**  
   Accounts like @balltime_ai use real fixture names plus exaggerated character labels like “Dictator Mbappe.” That format is much more TikTok-native than IG sports edits.

6. **Comment bait is baked into the premise.**  
   Prediction videos and “most-liked comment gets X” mechanics appear directly in captions. This is less polished than IG but more engagement-engineered.

7. **Underdog micro-narratives travel fast.**  
   Cape Verde nearly upsetting Argentina became a whole content cluster: highlights, Messi photo moments, robbery allegations, Vozinha hero edits, and Spanish/Portuguese commentary.

8. **Sound choice is part of genre identity.**  
   Official clips use original/broadcast audio or cinematic tracks. Fan edits use slowed montagem, phonk-like edits, Coldplay, and creator-original audios.

9. **TikTok favors immediate repost speed over polish.**  
   Many strong results were 1-18 hours old with rough captions, typos, or raw match labels. IG tends to reward cleaner aesthetic packaging; TikTok rewards being first and searchable.

10. **National-audience pockets are strong.**  
   Brazil/Portuguese TikTok, Spanish Messi discourse, Morocco pride edits, Egypt emotion clips, and Cape Verde underdog posts each formed their own dialect.
