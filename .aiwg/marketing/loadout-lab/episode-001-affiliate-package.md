## 7. Prompt Quality Diagnosis

The v1 generated prompts were compliant but underpowered:

- They described clean product-adjacent desk shots, not a GTA6-native moment.
- "Launch prep" appeared only as a mood, not as visible story pressure.
- The shots had UI labels but no retention device: no surprise, no reveal, no reason to watch the final frame.
- The assets could be stitched into a video, but they did not create a viral structure by themselves.
- They were category-safe, not audience-native. GTA6 viewers need a launch-night problem, a visual consequence, and a payoff.

Prompt-quality decision: **REVISE before further generation.**
