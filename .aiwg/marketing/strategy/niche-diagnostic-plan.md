# Niche Diagnostic Plan — Affiliate Content Pipeline

**Created**: 2026-07-03

## Principle

Start with a narrow niche, prove the repeatable pipeline, then clone the system into adjacent niches.

## Scoring dimensions

Score each candidate niche 1–5:

1. **Content velocity** — enough daily examples/trends to learn from.
2. **Visual demo strength** — product benefit can be shown in short-form video.
3. **Affiliate availability** — accessible offers/products with commission potential.
4. **Compliance risk** — lower is better; avoid medical/weight-loss/diagnostic claims.
5. **Persona fit** — AI personas can credibly present/demo without deception.
6. **Production complexity** — content can be generated without difficult physical proof.
7. **Path to owned e-commerce** — niche can later support our own store/private label.

## Candidate beachhead niches

| Niche | Why it may work | Main risk |
|---|---|---|
| Beauty tools/devices | Visual demos, clear before/after style content, good AI persona fit | Misleading results or unrealistic transformations |
| Skincare routine accessories | Routine content, high repeatability, less medical if framed properly | Claims can drift into acne/anti-aging treatment promises |
| Hair styling accessories/tools | Strong visual transformations, broad audience, demo-friendly | Need realistic hair physics and truthful outcomes |
| Sleep/relaxation lifestyle products | Routine-led content, broad demand | Medical/sleep disorder claims risk |
| Fitness/lifestyle accessories | Strong visuals and habit content | Weight-loss/body transformation claims risk |

## Research loop per niche

1. Gather 25–50 examples from TikTok/Instagram/Shop surfaces.
2. Tag each example by hook, persona, product type, proof mechanism, CTA, disclosure, claim risk, and engagement signal.
3. Identify 5 repeatable content templates.
4. Select 3 products/offers for test generation.
5. Generate 3 persona archetypes and 5 scripts per product.
6. Run compliance review before any publishing.
7. Publish/test or score creatives; capture results.
8. Decide: scale, iterate, or kill niche.

## Output of diagnostic phase

- Niche scorecard.
- Product shortlist.
- Persona archetype brief.
- Creative templates.
- Compliance guardrails.
- MVP pipeline requirements.
