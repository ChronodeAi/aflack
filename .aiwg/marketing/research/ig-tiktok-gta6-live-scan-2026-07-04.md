## Distilled insights added

- Debate CTAs tied to purchase/speculation moments drive comments.
- Link-hub/lead-magnet routing is visible on successful accounts.
- GTA6 posts that work often open with a concrete claim/comparison, add context, then ask a debate question.
- Leak/official-gameplay framing is common but risky; rewrite into rumor/speculation/commentary framing.
- Aesthetic editor accounts monetize through style consistency; Vice Signal needs a persistent visual signature.
